#  Sales Decline Analysis & Root Cause Identification

##  Project Overview

This project analyzes historical sales data to identify the key factors behind declining sales and profitability. Using **SQL, Excel, and Power BI**, the analysis focuses on uncovering trends, diagnosing business problems, and providing actionable recommendations.

---

##  Business Problem

The business has experienced fluctuations in sales and declining profitability. The objective of this project is to:

* Identify when and where sales decline occurred
* Analyze key drivers such as discounts, product performance, and regions
* Provide data-driven recommendations to improve business performance

---

##  Tools & Technologies

* **SQL** → Data cleaning, transformation, and analysis
* **Excel** → Data validation and exploratory analysis
* **Power BI** → Interactive dashboard and visualization

---

##  Dataset Description

The dataset contains transactional-level sales data including:

* Order details (Order ID, Order Date, Ship Date)
* Customer information (Customer ID, Segment, Region)
* Product details (Category, Sub-category, Product Name)
* Business metrics (Sales, Profit, Quantity, Discount)

---

##  Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Sales Trend Analysis

* Evaluated monthly sales performance
* Identified significant fluctuations and periods of decline

### 2. Regional Performance

* Compared sales and profit across regions
* Identified underperforming regions contributing to decline

### 3. Product & Category Analysis

* Analyzed category and sub-category performance
* Identified loss-making products

### 4. Discount Impact Analysis

* Evaluated relationship between discount and profit
* Identified over-discounting as a major issue

### 5. Customer Segment Analysis

* Compared sales across customer segments
* Identified dependency on specific segments

### 6. Operational Analysis

* Evaluated ship mode impact on profitability

---

##  Dashboard Features (Power BI)

* KPI Cards: Total Sales, Total Profit, Profit Margin
* Sales Trend (Time Series Analysis)
* Region-wise Performance
* Top & Bottom Products
* Discount vs Profit (Scatter Plot)
* Segment-wise Sales Distribution
* Ship Mode Analysis

---

##  Key Insights

* Sales show **high volatility**, indicating inconsistent demand patterns
* Certain regions significantly underperform compared to others
* Some product categories generate **high sales but low/negative profit**
* High discounts are strongly correlated with **profit loss**
* Business is heavily dependent on a single customer segment
* Shipping methods contribute to reduced profitability

---

##  Business Recommendations

* Optimize discount strategy to prevent margin loss
* Focus on high-performing products and eliminate loss-making ones
* Improve performance in underperforming regions
* Diversify customer base beyond dominant segments
* Optimize shipping and operational costs
* Implement better demand planning to stabilize sales trends

---

##  Project Outcome

This project demonstrates the ability to:

* Perform end-to-end data analysis

---

##  Conclusion

The analysis highlights that declining profitability is primarily driven by inefficient discounting strategies, underperforming product categories, and regional imbalances. Addressing these areas can significantly improve overall business performance.
